Who We Are

The network effect for data-driven medicine.

MIRTECH is the creator of a global data-sharing network – we work with customers from over 1000 institutions in over 200 countries.  Our network advances data-driven medicine to improve health outcomes and economics worldwide.

What We Do

We offer a cloud-based Software-as-a-Service platform — the MIRTECH platform — that enables healthcare institutions to get quick, robust insights from their data. We apply our technology to diseases such as cancer and inherited disorders, where combining genomic and phenotypic information is vital to support discoveries, treatment decisions, and drug development efforts.

Why we care

Our people are diverse. We come from more than 28 countries. For us, democratizing data-driven medicine is essential to ensure equal access to healthcare for patients worldwide.  We believe that when the right information is in the right hands, at the right time, and with the right level of confidence, better health solutions can be delivered and make life-changing differences, improving people’s health across the globe.

A word or two from our CEO

When founding MIRTECH, our goal is to create a tech company whose impact would significantly improve patients’ care. We’ve worked hard to develop a strong entrepreneurial spirit at MIRTECH, encouraging every employee to make a difference. Our corporate DNA, rooted in quality, precision, and robustness, is the key to our success and a promise of hope for millions worldwide.
